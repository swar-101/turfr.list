## Code of Conduct

### Purpose

turfr is built as a collaborative system where contributors work together with clarity, respect, and discipline.

This code of conduct defines the expected behavior to maintain a productive environment.


### Expected Behavior

* Communicate clearly and respectfully
* Focus on ideas, not individuals
* Be open to feedback and discussion
* Assume positive intent

### Unacceptable Behavior

The following will not be tolerated:

* Harassment, abuse, or intimidation in any form
* Sexualized language, content, or unwanted advances
* Trolling, baiting, or deliberately disruptive behavior
* Insulting, derogatory, or personal attacks
* Discrimination based on personal attributes
* Sharing private information without consent
* Repeated unconstructive or hostile interactions


### Collaboration Principles

* Prioritize clarity over ego
* Keep discussions constructive
* Support decisions with reasoning
* Respect the contribution process

### Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for ensuring that interactions remain constructive and aligned with the project’s goals.

Actions may include:

* Requesting changes in behavior
* Removing content or contributions
* Restricting or revoking participation

### Scope

This applies to all project spaces, including pull requests, issues, discussions, and related communication channels.
